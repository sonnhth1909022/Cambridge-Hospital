Frequently Asked Questioins

This is the FAQ questions. The most common questions will be answered here. Please check on this section first before you use the Help Desk section.

  • What are the clinic hours?

    Patients may schedule appointment by visiting the clinic between 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM Monday – Friday or by calling +24 7300 1866. The Emergency cases is available up to 8 PM.

  • What if i have to cancel my appointment?

    Sometimes the unexpected happens and you need to cancel your appointment make sure you call us or email us 1 day ago before cancellation.

  • How can I contact you?

    Please visit contact us page for all your queries and redressal of grievances. You can also contact us on +24 7300 1866 number.

  • What do i need to bring to my appointment?

    First appointment, please bring the following:

    + Insurance card

    + A picture Photo Any previous medical records (including test results from other clinics)

    + All current medications (with prescription )

    For Repeat Patient Appointments:

    + All current medications (with prescription )

  • What are the facilities available in the hospital?

    Our Hospitals are multispecialty hospitals and offer the complete range of medical and surgical specialties, with highly experienced doctors.

  • Do you have ambulance service?

    Yes, we have an ambulance service available 24/7.

  • Can I buy medicines from outside?

    All the medicines are provided by the hospital pharmacy only.
